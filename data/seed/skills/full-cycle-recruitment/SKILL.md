SKILL: FULL-CYCLE RECRUITMENT & TALENT ACQUISITION

SKILL ID:       HR-SK-001
ROLE:           HR Manager
CATEGORY:       Recruitment
DIFFICULTY:     Advanced
ESTIMATED TIME: 15-30 hours per role (end-to-end)

DESCRIPTION
Managing the complete recruitment lifecycle from workforce planning and job
requisition through sourcing, screening, interviewing, offer negotiation,
and onboarding handoff. This skill ensures the organisation attracts and
selects the best talent efficiently while maintaining compliance with
employment equity and anti-discrimination requirements.

STEP-BY-STEP PROCEDURE

STEP 1: WORKFORCE PLANNING & REQUISITION
  - Receive hiring request from department manager
  - Validate: Is this an approved position? Budget available? Headcount allocated?
  - Review or create the job description: Title, purpose, responsibilities,
    qualifications, competencies, reporting line, salary band
  - Define the candidate profile: Must-haves vs. nice-to-haves
  - Determine recruitment method: Internal, external, agency, or combination
  - Set timeline: Target start date minus 30-45 days = posting date

STEP 2: SOURCING & ADVERTISING
  - Post the role on: Company careers page, LinkedIn, job boards (Indeed,
    Glassdoor, industry-specific sites)
  - Activate the employee referral programme
  - Brief recruitment agencies (if using) with the specification and fee terms
  - Search talent pools: LinkedIn Recruiter, ATS database, alumni networks
  - For diversity: Post on targeted diversity job boards and networks
  - Target: Generate 20-50 qualified applications per role

STEP 3: SCREENING & SHORTLISTING
  - Review applications against the candidate profile criteria
  - Phone screen shortlisted candidates (15-20 min):
    * Confirm interest, availability, salary expectations, notice period
    * Assess communication and cultural fit
    * Verify key qualification requirements
  - Score candidates using a standardised rating matrix
  - Present shortlist of 5-8 candidates to the hiring manager
  - Schedule first-round interviews

STEP 4: INTERVIEWING
  First round (HR + Hiring Manager, 45-60 min):
  - Structured behavioural interview using STAR methodology
  - Assess: Technical competence, cultural fit, motivation, career goals
  - Score using the interview scorecard (1-5 per competency)

  Second round (Senior Leadership / Panel, 45-60 min):
  - Deeper technical or case-study assessment
  - Panel interview for senior roles
  - Presentation or skills test if applicable

  Final round (if needed):
  - Executive meet-and-greet for senior hires
  - Team compatibility session

STEP 5: ASSESSMENT & REFERENCE CHECKS
  - Administer assessments: Psychometric, technical skills, or cognitive tests
  - Conduct reference checks (minimum 2 professional references):
    * Verify employment dates, role, and reason for leaving
    * Ask: Strengths, areas for development, would you rehire?
  - Background checks: Criminal record, qualification verification, credit
    (as permitted by law and relevant to the role)

STEP 6: OFFER & NEGOTIATION
  - Prepare the offer: Title, salary, benefits, start date, reporting line
  - Ensure internal equity: Compare to existing employees in similar roles
  - Present verbal offer to preferred candidate
  - Handle negotiation: Salary, start date, benefits, remote work, signing bonus
  - Issue formal written offer letter upon verbal acceptance
  - Set acceptance deadline (typically 3-5 business days)

STEP 7: ONBOARDING HANDOFF
  - Confirm acceptance in writing; notify unsuccessful candidates
  - Initiate onboarding checklist with Office Administrator and IT
  - Schedule Day 1 orientation, team introductions, and training
  - Assign a buddy/mentor for the first 90 days
  - Set 30/60/90-day check-in milestones with the hiring manager

RECRUITMENT METRICS TO TRACK
- Time-to-hire: Days from requisition to accepted offer (target: < 30 days)
- Cost-per-hire: Total recruitment cost / number of hires
- Quality-of-hire: Performance rating at 6 and 12 months
- Offer acceptance rate: Target >= 85%
- Source effectiveness: Which channels produce the best hires?
- Diversity: % of shortlisted and hired candidates from underrepresented groups
- Candidate experience: Survey score >= 4.0/5.0

TOOLS & RESOURCES
- Applicant Tracking System (Greenhouse, Lever, Workable, BambooHR)
- LinkedIn Recruiter / LinkedIn Jobs
- Job boards (Indeed, Glassdoor, industry-specific)
- Interview scorecard templates
- Reference check questionnaire template
- Offer letter templates (legal-reviewed)
- Psychometric assessment tools (SHL, Hogan, Predictive Index)

QUALITY STANDARDS
- Job descriptions: Complete and bias-free language
- Screening consistency: All candidates scored on same criteria
- Interview fairness: Same questions for all candidates per role
- Compliance: Full documentation for employment equity reporting
- Candidate communication: Response within 5 business days at every stage
- Zero ghosting: Every applicant receives a final outcome notification
