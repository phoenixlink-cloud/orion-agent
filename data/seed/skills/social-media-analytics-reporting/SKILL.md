SKILL: SOCIAL MEDIA ANALYTICS & PERFORMANCE REPORTING

SKILL ID:       SM-SK-004
ROLE:           Social Media Content Creator
CATEGORY:       Analytics & Optimisation
DIFFICULTY:     Intermediate
ESTIMATED TIME: 3-4 hours per week (ongoing)

DESCRIPTION
Tracking, analysing, and reporting on social media performance across all
platforms to identify winning content patterns, optimise posting strategy,
and demonstrate ROI to stakeholders. This covers platform-native analytics,
cross-platform reporting, competitive benchmarking, and data-driven content
strategy refinement.

STEP-BY-STEP PROCEDURE

STEP 1: SET UP TRACKING INFRASTRUCTURE
  For each platform, ensure analytics access:
  - X: X Analytics (analytics.twitter.com) or X Pro
  - Reddit: Post/comment karma tracking, subreddit analytics
  - LinkedIn: Page analytics and post analytics
  - Instagram: Professional account with Insights enabled
  - TikTok: Pro/Business account with Analytics
  - YouTube: YouTube Studio analytics
  
  Cross-platform:
  - UTM parameters on all links shared from social
  - Link shortener (Bitly) for click tracking
  - Google Analytics: Track social referral traffic to website
  - CRM: Track social-sourced leads (attribution)

STEP 2: DEFINE KEY METRICS BY PLATFORM
  X (Twitter):
  - Impressions, engagement rate, profile visits, follower growth
  - Top tweets by impressions and engagement
  - Reply rate (indicates conversation quality)
  - Link clicks (traffic generation)

  Reddit:
  - Post karma and comment karma (growth trend)
  - Post upvote ratio and comment count
  - Subreddit-specific performance
  - Brand mention sentiment

  LinkedIn:
  - Impressions, engagement rate, follower growth
  - Click-through rate on content
  - Demographics of engaged audience
  - Company page analytics

  Instagram:
  - Reach, impressions, engagement rate
  - Reels views and completion rate
  - Story views and tap-forward rate
  - Follower demographics and growth

  TikTok:
  - Video views, average watch time, completion rate
  - Follower growth, profile views
  - Trending sounds used and performance
  - Traffic source breakdown (For You page vs. Following)

  YouTube Shorts:
  - Views, watch time, average view duration
  - Subscriber gain from Shorts
  - Click-through rate from impressions
  - Audience retention curve

STEP 3: WEEKLY PERFORMANCE REVIEW
  Every Monday, review the past week:
  1. Content scorecard: Performance of each post against benchmarks
  2. Top 3 performers: What made them work? (Format, topic, timing, hook)
  3. Bottom 3: Why did they underperform? Lessons learned
  4. Engagement quality: Are comments meaningful or just emoji reactions?
  5. Follower growth: Net new across all platforms
  6. Trending opportunities: What's emerging that we should jump on?
  7. Competitor scan: Any notable activity from competitors?

STEP 4: MONTHLY CROSS-PLATFORM REPORT
  Report structure:
  1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY: Overall social media health (1 paragraph)
  2. PLATFORM BREAKDOWN: Key metrics per platform with MoM trends
  3. TOP CONTENT: Best 5 pieces with metrics and analysis
  4. AUDIENCE GROWTH: Follower trends and demographic shifts
  5. ENGAGEMENT TRENDS: Rate changes, sentiment, community health
  6. COMPETITIVE BENCHMARKING: How we compare to 3-5 competitors
  7. WEBSITE TRAFFIC: Social referrals and conversion data
  8. LEAD ATTRIBUTION: Social-sourced MQLs and pipeline (if tracked)
  9. RECOMMENDATIONS: What to do more/less of next month
  10. CONTENT CALENDAR PREVIEW: Next month's planned themes

STEP 5: COMPETITIVE BENCHMARKING
  Track 3-5 competitor accounts on each platform:
  - Posting frequency and content types
  - Engagement rates (compare to your own)
  - Follower growth rate
  - Content themes and messaging
  - What's working for them that you could adapt?
  - What gaps exist that you could fill?

  Tools: Socialblade, Phlanx, native platform analytics, manual tracking

STEP 6: OPTIMISE BASED ON DATA
  Monthly strategy adjustments:
  - POSTING TIMES: Shift to times that show highest engagement
  - CONTENT MIX: Increase formats that perform well, reduce weak ones
  - PLATFORM PRIORITY: Allocate more time to platforms with best ROI
  - HASHTAG STRATEGY: Refresh hashtags based on reach and engagement data
  - AUDIENCE TARGETING: Adjust content for the audience that's actually engaging
  - EXPERIMENTATION: Reserve 20% of content for testing new formats/topics

TOOLS & RESOURCES
- Native analytics: X Analytics, Instagram Insights, TikTok Analytics,
  YouTube Studio, LinkedIn Analytics
- Cross-platform: Sprout Social, Hootsuite Analytics, Buffer Analyze
- Competitive: Socialblade, Phlanx, Brandwatch
- Website: Google Analytics 4 (social referral tracking)
- Reporting: Google Data Studio, Power BI, Canva (for visual reports)
- Link tracking: Bitly, UTM.io
- Spreadsheet: Monthly tracking template (Google Sheets / Excel)

QUALITY STANDARDS
- Weekly review: Completed every Monday without fail
- Monthly report: Delivered by working day 5
- Data accuracy: All metrics verified against platform-native sources
- Competitive tracking: Updated monthly for all tracked competitors
- Actionable insights: Every report includes 3+ specific recommendations
- ROI tracking: Social-sourced leads and traffic attributed in CRM/GA
